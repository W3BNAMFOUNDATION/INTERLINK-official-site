Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how INTERLINK protects your data.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how INTERLINK handles, processes, and protects user data in compliance with international standards such as GDPR, HIPAA, and enterprise information-security regulations.

2. Data Collection

INTERLINK does not collect personal data by default. All processing is performed locally on the user’s machine through the local Cognition Engine.

  • No cloud storage
  • No telemetry
  • No analytics unless explicitly enabled

3. Local-Only Processing

All AI inference, vectorization, and ingest operations occur on the user’s local hardware. Data is never transmitted externally unless the user manually integrates external APIs.

4. User Control

  • All data stays under the user's full control
  • Data can be deleted at any time
  • No access is granted to INTERLINK or third parties

5. Third-Party Services

If users configure external LLMs (DeepSeek, Groq, OpenAI, etc.), data may be sent to those providers depending on the user’s configuration. This is optional and disabled by default.

6. Security Measures

  • AES-256 encryption for local components
  • Encrypted memory caching
  • Zero-fault sandbox execution
  • No persistent logs unless manually enabled

7. Policy Updates

INTERLINK may update this policy to comply with evolving regulations. All changes will be reflected on this page.
